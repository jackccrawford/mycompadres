## Three Service Tiers

### 1. Entrepreneur Program
**= Founder Focus**
- **For:** Early stage entrepreneurs feeling lost
- **Challenge:** Struggling to find or follow purpose
- **Solution:** Clarity and purpose - (re)capturing energy and passion
- **Method:** Introductory Engagement
  - Uncover/rediscover purpose
  - Drive ambitious 3 year plan
  - 90 day accountability cycles

### 2. Business Advisory
**= Commercial Clarity**
- **For:** Business owners finding disconnect between purpose and results
- **Challenge:** Business not tracking with vision
- **Solution:** Practical, tactical advice - commercial rigour
- **Method:** Unique Advisory
  - Wisdom
  - Creative Commercial Clarity
  - Curated Introductions
  - Opportunities

### 3. Growth Partnership
**= Optimisation & Growth**
- **For:** Business owners struggling to reach next level
- **Challenge:** Can't scale or break through ceiling
- **Solution:** Strategic vision - knowing where to go and how
- **Method:** Structured Framework
  - 12 pillars driving innovation
  - Accountability
  - All types of growth
  - Creating New Futures
